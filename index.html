1)What is Web Hosting?

Suppose you run a business and you need a shop or store to keep whatever your business has, similarly whatever material you put on your website like Video, Photo or Text Article A storage is required to keep everyone on the Internet, now a computer is connected to the Internet and kept on 24 hours a day and all your content is stored on it is called Host Computer or Web Host and this The process is called Web Hosting.

2)How does web hosting work?

The hosting service stores your website files on web servers (high level computers) When someone enters their address or URL into their browser to access your website, these web servers accept the customer's request and deliver a copy of your website files to their computer, resulting in the customer's computer. Your website opens

The Internet is the largest network in the world through which millions of computers and mobile devices are connected to each other. You may have noticed that even in your home when two computers are connected to each other, it also becomes a small Internet. But it is a private network but when a computer is connected to a public network or not the data contained in it can be used or accessed by anyone in public it becomes a part of the Internet You can call it Web server or Web host

These web servers are connected to the Internet 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, and these web servers provide you with space on your computer for rent to secure the content of your website and to display that content online on your website.

3)Types of web hosting

There are many types of hosting services available in the web hosting industry.
1. Shared Server Hosting
2. Virtual Private Server (VPS) hosting
3. Dedicated Server Hosting
4. Cloud hosting

What type of hosting you should take depends on the needs of your website as every business / website has different needs that you should choose the right hosting keeping in mind.

What is Difference between .com ,.in,.net.edu etc.?

There are many domain names available in market when you have to built up a website at that time there is many confusion that which domain name is best?which domain name is good for our website?what is difference between .com .in .ner .org etc.
   So it is very important to know that what is different between these?Which we have to buy? Once you buy a domain it is very difficult to change again.

.com

.com domain is international domain.It is one of the top level domain.if you have to rank your website on international level then .com domain is best choice for you.On international level your website will be rank if you buy a .com domain.But competition also very big.Because all world websites are there and your website is one of them.But ones your website get ranks then traffic comes on your website is all of country.You get most traffic on .com domain .therefore .com domain is best domain. .com Domain is most expensive domain.

.in

.in domain means .india if you have to rank your website in india only then .in best for you. .in domain generally buy to rank in india.International visitors also visit your website if you buy .in domain but most preference to indian visitors.So if you have to publish your website only in india then .in is best domain name for you.Competition is less there because your website rank on national level only.therefore it is easy to rank your website because competition is less.

.edu

.edu is top level domain..edu means .education.You cant buy it.It is used for educational purpose.Educational information is shared in this using this domain.If you have to buy it then you have to take permission of government.To use this domain you have to take permission of government. It is special domain.It is very difficult to buy this domain.It is expensive than other.It is very simple to rank your website because competition is very less than other.

.org

The domain name org is a generic top-level domain (gTLD) of the Domain Name System (DNS) used in the Internet. The name is truncated from organization.The domain is commonly used by schools, open-source projects, and communities, but also by some for-profit entitie.
